How to Attempt MDCAT Test

Complete guide to test strategy and execution

Section-wise Strategy

  • Biology: Focus on diagrams and processes
  • Chemistry: Practice numericals and reactions
  • Physics: Master formulas and units
  • English: Improve reading speed and vocabulary

    • Marking Scheme

    4 marks for correct answer, -1 for incorrect. Don't guess randomly.

    Only attempt when you can eliminate at least 2 options.

    Speed vs Accuracy

    Aim for 80% accuracy with good speed rather than 100% with incomplete paper.

    Complete all sections in mock tests to build stamina.

    Mock Test Pattern

    Take weekly full-length mock tests under exam conditions.

    Analyze mistakes and focus on weak areas.
