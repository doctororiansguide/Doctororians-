Tips for Better MDCAT Score

Strategic advice to maximize your marks

Time Management

Allocate specific time for each section. Spend no more than 1 minute per question initially.

Question Selection

Answer easy questions first, mark difficult ones, and return to them later.

Common Mistakes

Avoid reading too much into questions. Stick to what's explicitly stated.

Last-minute Tips

Review formulas and key concepts 1-2 days before exam, not new topics.

Memory Techniques

Use mnemonics and association techniques for remembering complex information.
